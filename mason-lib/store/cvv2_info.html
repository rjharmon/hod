<%flags> inherit => undef Credit Card Security Code
Visa, MasterCard & Discover  

Look on the back of your card, in or near the signature box. The last 4 digits of your card number will be shown, followed by the 3 digit code Security Code.

Sometimes the full card number is shown, as in the photo.
American Express  
Look for the 4 digit code printed on the front of your card just above and to the right of your main credit card number. This 4 digit number is your Security Code.

Note: On the Optima cards the 4 digit Security Code appears on the left border.
